A MULTI-DISCIPLINARY CARE APPROACH

Each patient benefits from an integrated breast care program that is evaluated from various medical perspectives. So, instead of the hassle of meeting different specialists in several clinics, NBCC offers consistently organized medical services, clear communication between physicians and patients, and most importantly, an interrupted continuity of care.

While Breast Cancer is a serious disease,
most patients go on to live a normal life

WITH EARLY DETECTION AND TREATMENT, A CURE CAN BE ACHIEVED FOR UP TO 95% OF THE CASES.

With this concept in mind, NBCC is dedicated to help reduce the rate of advanced Breast Cancer cases in the region, by educating women on the importance of early detection through regular screening and breast self-examination.
