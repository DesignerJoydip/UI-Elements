About the Author

Kristine Grant, MFT, as a psychotherapist, has provided counseling support for individuals, couples and families for well over twenty years. She also has an in-depth background as an educational psychologist. Kristine is the creator of the Relationshift® Process and Inspired Heart® Letters, a unique and powerful approach that positively transforms any matter of the heart. Currently, as a Relationship Coach, people can call upon Kristine’s help from anywhere in the US and beyond. She is a national speaker and has appeared on numerous radio and television shows including CBS, ABC, Fox, and the CW Network. For more information visit: www.inspiredheartletters.com
