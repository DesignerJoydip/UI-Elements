Al Murooj

The exclusive Al Murooj district on the coast of the Red Sea offers a range of distinctive properties that reflect your own style and life journey. A serene setting for a wide range of recreational facilities, Al Murooj is a gated community providing the ultimate privacy and security.

Why Al Murooj?

Beach Community

The Beach Community at Al Murooj provides the most exclusive coastal properties available designed to meet your exact requirement for a stunning, original home on the Red Sea Coast

GOLF COMMUNITY

Enjoying sweeping views over the grassy fairways of the Royal Greens Golf and Country Club, the Al Murooj Golf Community a unique and highly desirable place to enjoy a world class lifestyle.

AL MANAZEL

The Al Manazel district in Al Murooj is a high-end community of spacious, premium homes, giving you the ultimate combination of exclusive living with the best in privacy and security.

WORLD CLASS AMENITIES

The Al Murooj District gives you the closest proximity and convenient access to KAEC's world class recreational and leisure amenities, from the award-winning Royal Greens Golf and Country Club to the outstanding Esmeralda Health and Wellness Center
