Dedicated Remote Staffing

Finding qualified technical talent is no easy task, especially in the MSP industry. We understand the struggle. As your expert MSP talent partner, we know how to find the best team members, train them in the MSP Way of delivering service, and create a culture of WOW to support and retain them.

How it works:

When an MSP has a need, they simply call us to start the process.

  • Resource, competency and skills mapping aligns candidates with needs
  • ITBD conducts Zoom interviews, additional testing if required
  • You select best-fit candidates—evaluating tech, soft and social skills and culture/values
  • Alignment
  • We handle onboarding as soon as you make your choices
  • Can also pick from our bench of 30 engineers at the ready

Direct Hire Staffing

When you can’t find the right engineer to scale your MSP practice quickly, let ITBD help. We understand the qualifications for an effective MSP engineer and have years of hiring experience, with more than 450 ITBD team members internally, and more than 20,000 interviews completed in five years.

How
it works:

  • Complete a job order form
  • Our sourcing team searches for your ideal team player
  • Within 2 weeks you receive information on pre-screened candidates
  • After selecting someone you like, we onboard them into your team
  • 90-day guarantee: If you aren’t happy, we’ll gladly find another candidate
  • 3 easy payments of sourcing fee
